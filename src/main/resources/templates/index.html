Our History

Welcome to BrushUp Painting Company!
John Paintgood started BrushUp Painting Company in 1946, where he happily took any work he could, from painting fences to commercial and residential buildings. John was famous in his home of Danville and the surrounding tri state area for his catch phrase of Brush Up To A Brighter Future And Home!

As John expanded his business he hired on more painters to help him handle the overwhelming amount of contracts he received.
Of course, John continued to work as a painter. In 1953 he painted the home of Jane Gray and made good on his promise to brighten her house and future. In fact, he did such a good job, she decided to marry him.

BrushUp Painting Company is still alive and well today! Our motto is exactly the same as before:

Brush Up To A Brighter Future And Home!
